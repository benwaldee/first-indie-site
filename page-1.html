The Square Game

What is this?

The square game is a simple puzzle game designed using basic HTML and CSS. It matches given specifications for an assigned project while also thinking outside of the box . . . or square. Good luck and have fun!

How to play:

The first thing you will notice is the large amount of pages listed in the nav bar above. You are on the first page, and pages 2-4 contain the puzzle in sequential order. Only move to the next page when you have completed the previous, or else you will not have very much fun. Hidden in one of the remaining links is the reward for completing the game, however you will not be able to find this reward without important information gained by completing pages 2-4. When you are ready to begin, click on page 2 in the navigation bar. You got this!
